Mangal Namdev Darsewad is the owner and proprietor of Jayvijay Enterprises. She founded this company with a clear vision — to provide comprehensive commercial and industrial solutions to businesses across Nanded and the Marathwada region.

Operating from Datta Krupa Complex, HUDCO, Nanded, the company serves clients through two specialized divisions: Sales (hardware supply) and Services (regulatory compliance and environmental consultancy).
