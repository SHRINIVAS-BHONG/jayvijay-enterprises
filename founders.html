OWNER & PROPRIETOR

Mangal Namdev Darsewad

📋 Shops Act Reg. No.: 2541600320645296 📍 HUDCO, Nanded — Established 2025

Mangal Namdev Darsewad is the owner and proprietor of Jayvijay Enterprises. She founded this company with a clear vision — to provide comprehensive commercial and industrial solutions to businesses across Nanded and the Marathwada region.

Operating from Datta Krupa Complex, HUDCO, Nanded, the company serves clients through two specialized divisions: Sales (hardware supply) and Services (regulatory compliance and environmental consultancy).

SALES & SERVICES MANAGER

Jay Darsewad

💼 Sales Division 📋 Services Division

Jay Darsewad manages both divisions of Jayvijay Enterprises. He oversees the hardware sales, procurement, and client relationships for the Sales division, ensuring quality products and reliable service to builders and contractors across Nanded.

For the Services division, Jay heads the consultancy and regulatory compliance wing, bringing in-depth knowledge of Maharashtra industrial laws, MPCB regulations, environmental compliance, and guiding businesses through complex approval processes.
