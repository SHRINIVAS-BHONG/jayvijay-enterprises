ALL SERVICES

What We Offer

🏭

Factory License

Complete assistance for factory registration and licensing under the Maharashtra Factories Act, 1948. Covers new registration, renewal, and amendments.

  • New factory registration
  • License renewal & amendment
  • Plan approval assistance
  • Compliance documentation
🌊

MPCB / CPCB Approvals

Maharashtra and Central Pollution Control Board consent to establish (CTE) and consent to operate (CTO) for industrial and commercial units.

  • Consent to Establish (CTE)
  • Consent to Operate (CTO)
  • Renewal of consents
  • Compliance reports & returns
🏗️

MIDC Land & Transfer Work

End-to-end assistance for MIDC industrial plot allotment, transfer of plots, sub-letting permissions, and all MIDC documentation and correspondence.

  • New plot allotment
  • Plot transfer assistance
  • Sub-letting permissions
  • MIDC correspondence & follow-up
🔥

Fire NOC

No Objection Certificate from the Fire Department for factories, warehouses, commercial establishments, and all industrial units as required by law.

  • New Fire NOC application
  • NOC renewal
  • Fire safety plan preparation
  • Liaison with Fire Department
⚖️

Labour Law Licensing

Comprehensive support for all labour law registrations and compliance requirements under central and state laws applicable to your establishment.

  • PF & ESIC Registration
  • Contract Labour License
  • Shops & Establishment Registration
  • Professional Tax Registration
🏢

MSME नोंदणी

Registration and certification for Micro, Small & Medium Enterprises on the Udyam portal. Unlock government schemes, subsidies, and priority lending benefits.

  • Udyam Registration (new)
  • Udyog Aadhaar migration
  • Certificate & documentation
  • Benefit scheme guidance
📊

Environmental Impact Assessment

Preparation of EIA reports and environmental clearances required for projects under the Environment Protection Act and EIA Notification 2006.

  • Screening & scoping
  • EIA report preparation
  • Public hearing support
  • MoEFCC clearance filing
♻️

Waste Management Solutions

Planning and compliance for solid waste, hazardous waste, and bio-medical waste management as per CPCB/MPCB guidelines and Solid Waste Management Rules.

  • Hazardous waste authorization
  • Waste disposal planning
  • Compliance returns filing
  • Waste audit & reporting
💧

Water Management Solutions

Water audits, water balance studies, groundwater NOC, and water conservation planning for industries to meet regulatory requirements and reduce water usage.

  • Water audit & balance study
  • Groundwater NOC (CGWA)
  • Water conservation planning
  • Regulatory reporting
🚰

Effluent Management Solutions

ETP design guidance, effluent treatment monitoring, and trade effluent compliance for industries discharging wastewater as per MPCB standards.

  • ETP design guidance
  • Effluent sampling & monitoring
  • Trade effluent compliance
  • ZLD planning support
🌱

Sustainability Audits

Comprehensive sustainability performance evaluation covering energy, water, waste, emissions, and social aspects to align with ESG standards and reporting frameworks.

  • Energy & resource audit
  • ESG performance review
  • Sustainability action plan
  • Annual sustainability report
🌍

Carbon Footprint Analysis

Greenhouse gas (GHG) emission inventories, Scope 1/2/3 calculations, and carbon reduction strategy development for industries and businesses.

  • GHG emission inventory
  • Scope 1, 2 & 3 calculations
  • Carbon reduction roadmap
  • Net-zero strategy guidance
